Baked wings

Ready, set... WINGS!!!

What is it about chicken wings? Why can't we resist them?? That is one of life's mysteries. Just to double-check this... Here's a recipe for you.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, pressed
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 10 chicken wings

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  2. Step 2: Combine the olive oil, garlic, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a large, resealable bag; seal and shake to combine. Add the chicken wings; reseal and shake to coat. Arrange the chicken wings on a baking sheet
  3. Step 3: Cook the wings in the preheated oven 1 hour, or until crisp and cooked through
